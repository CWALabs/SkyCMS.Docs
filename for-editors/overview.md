# Editor Guide Overview

Editors handle day-to-day content operations in SkyCMS: drafting, reviewing, publishing, scheduling, and retiring content.

## When to use this page

Use this guide when you need to:

- understand the end-to-end editorial lifecycle,
- map tasks to the right editor tools,
- confirm role boundaries before handoff.

## Editorial Workflow

For most pages and posts:

1. [Create](./creating-articles.md) an article.
2. Save and revise in draft.
3. Preview and review.
4. Publish now or [schedule](./page-scheduling.md) publication.
5. Update and republish as needed.
6. Unpublish, delete, or trash when content is no longer needed.

Quick reference: [Article Lifecycle Quick Reference](./article-lifecycle-reference.md)

## Tooling Map

- [Visual Editor](./visual-editor.md): inline editing in page context.
- [Page Builder](./page-builder.md): visual drag-and-drop page construction.
- [Code Editor](./code-editor.md): precise HTML/CSS/JavaScript editing.
- [AI Assistant](./ai-assistant.md): AI help in Monaco, CKEditor, and standalone help chat.
- [Image Editing](./image-editing.md): crop, annotate, resize, and optimize assets.
- [File Manager](./file-manager.md): upload, organise, and manage website assets.
- [Page Scheduling](./page-scheduling.md): schedule publication windows.

## Role Boundaries

- Editor: content creation, revision, scheduling, publishing.
- Site Builder: layouts/templates and presentation structure.
- Developer: platform customization and integrations.
